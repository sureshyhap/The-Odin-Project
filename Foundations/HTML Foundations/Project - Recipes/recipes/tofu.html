Baked Tofu

What is tofu?

Tofu is made from coagulated soy milk that is pressed into solid white blocks that have different levels of firmness ranging from silken to super firm. It can be used as a meat substitute. It has its origins in China.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • Soy Sauce
  • Agave Nectar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Ginger
  • Tofu

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a large baking sheet with cooking spray.

  2. Combine soy sauce, agave, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil together in a bowl.

  3. Press tofu pieces to release as much water as possible. Arrange them onto baking sheet and brush them with some of the soy mixture.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Flip and brush with the rest of the soy mixture. Sprinkle sesame seeds. Continue baking until crisp (about 30 more minutes).

  5. Remove from oven and allow to cool.

  6. Enjoy!
