Palak Paneer

Palak paneer is an Indian dish that includes paneer (cottage cheese) and palak (puréed spinach). It is usually served hot alongside boiled rice, roti, naan, etc.

Ingredients

  • Ghee
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Tomato paste
  • Ginger
  • Garam Masala
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Water
  • Chopped spinach
  • Paneer
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Melt ghee over low heat. Stir in garlic and cumin; cook until garlic softens (about 3 minutes). Add tomato paste, ginger, garam masala, salt, onion, and water. Increase heat to medium and stir until tomato paster dissolves. Simmer slowly for an hour, adding water as needed to maintain a sauce-like consistency.

  2. Stir in spinach and cook until hot (about 5 minutes). Add paneer and cook for an additional 5 minutes or until hot (whichever comes first). Serve with sprinkled cilantro and more garam masala.
