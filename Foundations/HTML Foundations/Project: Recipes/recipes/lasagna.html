Lasagna

What is lasagna?

Lasagna is a traditonal Italian pasta made of layers of flat sheets. These layers can include a variety of cheeses such as
mozarella, parmesan, and ricotta, sauces such as tomato and bechamel, ground meats, vegetables, seasonings, and spices.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna in the following order:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then the top with the remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
